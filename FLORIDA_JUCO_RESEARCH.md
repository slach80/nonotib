## Analysis for Noah's Recruiting

### Why Florida JUCOs Don't Fit Current Strategy

1. **No Local Options**
   - Tampa Bay has zero JUCO men's soccer programs
   - Nearest is 100+ miles (not commutable)

2. **Strong 4-Year Options Already Identified**
   - Tampa Bay has 7 excellent 4-year programs in target list
   - D1: USF
   - D2: Tampa, Florida Southern, Saint Leo, Eckerd, Lynn
   - NAIA: Southeastern, Warner

3. **Academic Profile**
   - Noah is college prep track (SAT prep, strong GPA)
   - Direct 4-year admission is appropriate path
   - JUCO typically for students needing:
     - Academic improvement before 4-year eligibility
     - More physical development/playing time
     - Cost savings (2 years CC, then transfer)

4. **MLS Next Background**
   - Noah's Sporting KC Academy experience positions him for 4-year programs
   - JUCO is more common for non-academy players

### When Florida JUCO Might Make Sense

Consider JUCO only if:
- ✅ Noah needs academic improvement before D1/D2 eligibility
- ✅ Needs more playing time to develop before 4-year level
- ✅ Family willing to relocate or Noah boarding away from home
- ✅ Scholarship concerns (JUCO typically cheaper than 4-year private)
- ✅ Late recruiting timeline (senior year, no 4-year offers yet)

**For Class of 2028 (Noah):** Still has 2+ years to secure 4-year commitments. JUCO not necessary at this stage.
