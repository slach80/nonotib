Recruiting Feasibility — What It Means

High Feasibility

Noah's MLS Next AD background, 3.5 GPA, and KC area profile make him a realistic recruiting target. With a highlight reel and direct coach outreach, expect interest and likely scholarship conversation.

Medium Feasibility

Program is competitive — Noah is in range but needs strong showcase appearances, good film, and early outreach (by sophomore fall). Getting a trial or ID camp invite is the key first step.

Low Feasibility

Reach program — either soccer level significantly exceeds Noah's current exposure or academic standards are above average. Worth contacting but should not anchor the recruiting strategy.
