## When KC JUCO Makes Sense for Noah

### Good Reasons to Consider JUCO

1. **Academic Development**
   - Needs to improve GPA before 4-year eligibility
   - Wants to boost SAT scores while playing
   - Needs more coursework to meet NCAA initial eligibility

2. **Athletic Development**
   - Wants more playing time immediately (4-year might redshirt)
   - Needs to develop physically before D1/D2 level
   - Late bloomer, still growing into position

3. **Financial Reasons**
   - 2 years JUCO (cheaper) + 2 years 4-year = cost savings
   - JUCO scholarships often full-ride (cover tuition/fees/books)
   - Can live at home (JCCC or KCKCC) = no room/board costs

4. **Late Recruiting Timeline**
   - Senior year, no 4-year offers yet
   - JUCO recruiting extends later than 4-year programs
   - Can commit in spring before graduation

5. **Injury Recovery**
   - Serious injury junior/senior year
   - Needs playing time to show healthy/ready
   - JUCO as "prove it" year before 4-year

### Why Noah Might NOT Need JUCO (Current Assessment)

✅ **Academics:** College prep track, preparing for SAT  
✅ **Playing Time:** MLS Next Academy background = 4-year ready  
✅ **Timeline:** Class of 2028 = 2+ years to secure 4-year commitment  
✅ **Visibility:** Portfolio, SportsRecruits, game film = good exposure  
✅ **Development:** Competing at MLS Next level = physically ready

**Verdict:** JUCO is backup plan, not primary path for Noah
