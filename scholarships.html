Missouri Bright Flight — Priority #1

Bright Flight is the single highest-value scholarship Noah can unlock with effort. Missouri awards up to $3,000/year (renewable 4 years = $12,000 total) to Missouri residents who score in the top 3% of all Missouri ACT test-takers — approximately ACT 31+. Noah has not yet taken the ACT, meaning this is fully attainable. A strong ACT score also strengthens every other scholarship application and college admissions package. Register at act.org — next available test dates should be booked as soon as possible.
